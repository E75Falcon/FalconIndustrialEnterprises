About Us

Falcon Industrial Enterprises is a partnership firm found in 1980 by Lt.Col.Mukhtar Singh Khaira, with a vision to manufacture quality components for vehicles & tractors industry.

During these years we have acquired an excellent track record in terms of Quality, Schedule Adherence & Technology Upgradation. Customer satisfaction in terms of quality & timely supplies always remained on top priority in our working environment.

We specialize in the manufacturing of a wide range of high-quality components, including Shafts, Clutch Pedal Mounting Brackets, Forks, Greasing Tubes, Clevises, Rods, Bush Assemblies and all types of precision-turned shafts.
